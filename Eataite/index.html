Image Order for Slack

Would you like to take a picture and place a food order?

Install it on Your Slack Team

Setting up Image Order in Slack is simple. Just click the button to log in to your Slack account and choose a team!

Once you install the bot, you should be able to use the slash command on your team chat room. Just type /imageorder followed by your thoughts, emotions, or feelings.
